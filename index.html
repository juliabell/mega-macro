Methodology & Notes

Framework: This is a long-only, quarterly-rebalance portfolio. No shorts, no leverage, no tactical trades. Positions are sized for a minimum 3-month hold period and evaluated only at rebalance dates.

Weight derivation: Only LT and Secular timeframe signals are used for sizing. Assets with multi-analyst agreement receive higher weights (max 10%). Single-analyst themes are capped at 3-5%. ST/MT signals inform watchlist timing, not current allocation.

Conviction scale: Each filled dot = 1 analyst with an active LT or Secular bullish call. 5 dots = unanimous agreement across all tracked analysts.

Cash strategy: 52% cash is a feature, not a bug. When 4/5 analysts say avoid equities and 3/5 say avoid bonds, the opportunity cost of cash is low and the optionality value is high. T-Bills provide >4% carry. Cash will be deployed gradually as watchlist setups trigger at future rebalance dates.
